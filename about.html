Our Story

Where ancient craftsmanship meets modern culinary artistry

Our Family Story

We started our olive wood utensil business last year, importing premium olive wood from our family farm in Tunisia. We then process this wood in Oregon City, crafting each piece by hand in our family-owned workshop. This is a family operation through and through, where we bring you the highest quality kitchen utensils made from sustainable olive wood.

The Artisan Process

Every piece in our collection is the result of meticulous craftsmanship, combining ancient techniques with modern precision. Our process honors both the wood and the hands that shape it.

01

Wood Selection & Preparation

Our artisans begin by carefully selecting olive wood from our sustainable family farm in Tunisia. Each piece is chosen for its unique grain pattern, density, and natural beauty. The wood is then imported and further processed in Oregon City.

Sustainably sourced from ancient groves
2-year natural drying process
Individual grain pattern assessment
02

Design & Layout

Before carving begins, our master artisans study each piece of wood, envisioning how its natural grain will influence the final design. Traditional templates are combined with modern ergonomic principles to ensure both beauty and functionality.

Grain-guided design approach
Ergonomic functionality testing
Balance and proportion analysis
03

Hand Carving & Sculpting

This is where art meets craftsmanship. Using traditional hand tools passed down through generations, our artisans carefully carve each piece, working in harmony with the wood's natural properties to create forms that are both beautiful and functional.

Traditional hand tools only
3-5 days per piece
Family craftsmanship
04

Sanding, Finishing & Quality Control

The final stages involve meticulous sanding through multiple grits, followed by our signature food-safe finish. Each piece undergoes rigorous quality testing to ensure it meets our exacting standards.

8-stage sanding process
Food-safe natural oil finish
Multi-point quality testing

Sustainability at Our Core

We believe that true luxury comes from respecting our planet. Our olive wood is sourced from sustainable groves where trees are carefully managed and harvested responsibly. For every tree used in our creations, we plant new olive trees to ensure the legacy continues.

The natural antimicrobial properties of olive wood mean our utensils require no chemical treatments, making them safe for your family and gentle on the environment.
