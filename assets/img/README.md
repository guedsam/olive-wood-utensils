## Image Specifications

### Dimensions
- **Product cards:** 400x300px (4:3 ratio)
- **Hero images:** 1200x800px (3:2 ratio)
- **Detail galleries:** 800x600px (4:3 ratio)
- **Thumbnails:** 150x150px (1:1 ratio)

### Quality Guidelines
- High resolution (minimum 150 DPI)
- Professional photography with proper lighting
- Consistent color temperature (natural daylight preferred)
- Clean, neutral backgrounds or contextual settings
- Focus on wood grain and craftsmanship details

### File Formats
- **Primary:** JPEG (.jpg) for photographs
- **Graphics:** PNG (.png) for images with transparency
- **Icons:** SVG (.svg) for scalable graphics

### Naming Convention
- Use kebab-case (lowercase with hyphens)
- Descriptive names that match product names
- Include view numbers for multiple angles (e.g., spatula-1.jpg, spatula-2.jpg)

## Placeholder Usage

Until actual product images are available, the website will display placeholder content. For production, replace all image references with actual product photography that showcases:

- The unique grain patterns of olive wood
- The craftsmanship and attention to detail
- The natural antimicrobial properties (subtle highlighting)
- The ergonomic design and functionality
- The luxury, artisanal nature of each piece

## Photography Tips

1. **Lighting:** Use soft, diffused natural light to highlight wood grain
2. **Angles:** Shoot from multiple angles to show functionality
3. **Styling:** Include contextual shots (food preparation, table settings)
4. **Details:** Close-up shots of wood grain, carving marks, and finishes
5. **Consistency:** Maintain consistent lighting and angles across all products

## Image Credits

All product images should be:
