Frequently Asked Questions

How do I care for my olive wood utensils?

Hand wash with mild soap and warm water. Occasionally treat with food-safe mineral oil to maintain the wood's natural luster. Avoid soaking or placing in dishwasher.

Do you offer custom engraving?

Yes! We offer custom engraving services for special occasions. Contact us for details and pricing.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original condition. Custom orders are final sale.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for retailers and restaurants. Please contact us for more information.
