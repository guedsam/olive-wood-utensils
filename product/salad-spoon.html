The Story Behind the Salad Spoon

The salad spoon is the workhorse of fresh food preparation. It needs to be substantial enough to handle hearty ingredients, yet gentle enough to preserve the delicate textures of fresh greens. Our salad spoons represent decades of refinement in this essential kitchen tool.

Our salad spoons are carved from olive wood that has been naturally seasoned for at least four years. This aging process creates the perfect density for the wide bowl that can efficiently mix large quantities of ingredients while maintaining the integrity of each component.

"A salad spoon should be an extension of the chef's intention—strong enough to command, gentle enough to nurture. It's the bridge between preparation and presentation." — Isabella Rossi, Culinary Director

The elongated handle provides optimal leverage for mixing in large bowls, while the wide, shallow bowl ensures even distribution of dressings and seasonings. Each salad spoon develops its own character over time, the wood taking on a beautiful patina that tells the story of countless vibrant, fresh meals.

Caring for Your Olive Wood Salad Spoon

Hand Wash Only

Gently wash with mild soap and warm water immediately after use. Pay special attention to the bowl area where food residues collect.

Dry Thoroughly

Pat dry with a soft cloth and allow to air dry completely before storing. Never put in dishwasher or soak in water.

Monthly Conditioning

Apply food-safe mineral oil monthly to maintain the wood's natural luster and prevent drying, especially in the bowl area.
