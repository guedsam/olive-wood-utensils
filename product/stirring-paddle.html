The Story Behind the Stirring Paddle

The stirring paddle is the workhorse of the serious kitchen. It must be substantial enough to handle thick doughs and heavy mixtures, yet gentle enough to scrape bowls clean without damaging delicate surfaces. Our stirring paddles represent decades of refinement in this essential cooking tool.

Our stirring paddles are carved from olive wood that has been naturally seasoned for at least five years. This aging process creates the perfect density for the wide blade that can efficiently mix large quantities while maintaining the integrity of ingredients and cookware.

"A stirring paddle should be an extension of the cook's intention—powerful enough to command, gentle enough to nurture. It's the bridge between effort and artistry." — Lucia Marinelli, Master Baker

The elongated handle provides optimal leverage for mixing in large bowls and deep pots, while the wide, flat blade ensures complete ingredient incorporation. Each stirring paddle develops its own character over time, the wood taking on a beautiful patina that tells the story of countless culinary creations.

Caring for Your Olive Wood Stirring Paddle

Hand Wash Only

Gently wash with mild soap and warm water immediately after use. The wide blade requires thorough cleaning to prevent residue buildup.

Dry Thoroughly

Pat dry with a soft cloth and allow to air dry completely before storing. Never put in dishwasher or soak in water.

Monthly Conditioning

Apply food-safe mineral oil monthly to maintain the wood's natural luster and prevent drying, especially on the blade surface.
